About PhishGuard

Protecting users from phishing attacks with advanced AI technology

Our Story

PhishGuard was born from the growing need to protect individuals and organizations from sophisticated phishing attacks. With cyber threats becoming increasingly complex and targeted, traditional security measures are no longer sufficient.

Our team of cybersecurity experts and AI researchers came together to develop a comprehensive solution that combines the power of machine learning with real-time threat intelligence to detect and prevent phishing attacks before they can cause harm.

Today, PhishGuard serves as a trusted shield against phishing attempts, offering three powerful detection methods: AI-powered email analysis, QR code scanning with real-time camera support, and direct URL analysis with comprehensive security checks.

Our Mission

We are committed to making the internet a safer place by providing accessible, accurate, and reliable phishing detection tools that protect users from cyber threats while maintaining their privacy and security.
